About Me

I'm Nicklaus Meadows and I am originally from Columbus, Ohio. Currently, my wife and I are living in a suburb of Columbus called Canal Winchester. We moved out here about 10 years ago after we met at OSU, while we both we're studying commercial photography. Since we've been out in Canal we have been developing a small homestead setup that we both really enjoy working on. We have some chickens, quail, turkeys, ducks and a nice sized garden. Professionally, I work for a chemical company in Columbus as a web designer and SEO analyst. Prior to my current position I worked on the front-end side of a school system's website and a commercial photographer for Lane Bryant and a local diamond company. I occasionally still pick up photography jobs on the side if it is the right fit, but mainly I have been shooting film for the enjoyment of the process. My wife and I really enjoy traveling, and in 2025 we're going on a trip to SC with a friend of mine to just take some nice photos and relax. In my spare time I really enjoy working on my old Jeep Wrangler. It was my dad's and I kept it after he passed. The Jeep is old enough to legally drink and driving through Ohio winter's for that long has really taken a toll on the frame and pretty much everything facing the pavement. So over the past few years I've been trying to restore it to the best of my abilities. Luckily, youtube is a huge help since a lot of people have owned these Jeeps and love making tutorial videos. Next summer, I hope to have it in well enough shape to take out for a nice cruise around the country.
